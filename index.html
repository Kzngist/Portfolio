About Me

I have been a video gamer since before I went to kindergarten. Being passionate in the whole gaming industry, I started my journey as a game developer in early 2018, and have created games of various genres using Unity. I always pursue perfection and originality, and thus have dived deep into most aspects of game development, including advanced 3D/2D content creation, mobile development, AI, audio/video compositing, UI/UX engineering, shading, VFX, etc. Among which I excel at high-quality game-ready character creation, and have been utilizing original assets across the many games I have created. I love challenges and is constantly learning, and I am eager to join a cooperative environment to further elaborate my talents

Education

Bachelor of Science (Honours) - Computer Science, Brock University
